Program Objectives

To expose students to real-life problems solving.

To bridge the gap between academics and industry.

To inculcate entrepreneurial and problem solving skills to students.

What You'll Get

Business Diagnosis & Coaching

One-on-one coaching to identify growth barriers and craft solutions.

Investment Readiness Training

Financial modeling, pitch deck design, and due diligence preparation.

•Market Access Support

Partnership facilitation, B2B/B2G networking, and digital go-to-market strategies.

Customer Validation Labs

Tools and workshops to gather user feedback and iterate on your offerings.

Founders Roundtables

Peer learning circles where founders exchange challenges, wins, and insights.

Demo Day

Culmination event where founders Pitch to investors, incubators, and accelerators.

Expected Outcomes

  • Refined business models and clear growth strategies for your startup
  • At least 30% of participants access follow-on funding or investment
  • Increased startup survival rates and job creation in the local economy
  • Access to Start-Up Alumni Circle with ongoing support and pitch practice
  • Investors network and VC partnership agreements
